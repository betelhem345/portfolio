About Me

Hello, my name is Bethlehem, I have a background in marketing management. Currently, I am a software development student in the Year Up program. My goal is to get my programming certificate and integrate my marketing management expertise with computer programming to create a successful carrier path. The marketing profession needs IT professionals who know the marketing field to create a system that works best with the field. My dream is to be that bridge that connects the two.

I was born and raised in East Africa, Ethiopia. Growing up in a very diverse ethnic group gave me an opportunity to see things from a broader perspective.

My hobbies are reading, travelling and trying new things. My faith and my family are two things that are very dear to me.

I believe hard work and dedication will get you to where you want to be. Never give up on your dreams stay strong through hard times and success will be yours.

Let's all help eachother and grow together to create a better tomorrow for everyone
